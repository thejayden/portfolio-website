SCDF x IBM Hackathon: Elderly Care Smart Solution

My maiden hackathon with a group of friends from NTU. The problem we detected: With more elderlies living alone and isolated in their homes in Singapore, there is a high risk of underdetection and a lack of help rendered to these elderlies should an emergency occur. The proposed solution: Use smart watch IoT device to detect fall risk/heartrate with machine learning and an inbuilt chatbot to call for emergency responders.
